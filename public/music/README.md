# Music & Sound Effects Folder

## Background Music for Confession

**File:** `our-song.mp3`
- Place your romantic song here
- It will auto-play when the confession slideshow starts
- Music pauses when slideshow is paused
- Music continues playing during the proposal

## Fireworks Sound Effect

**File:** `fireworks.mp3`
- Place fireworks sound effect here (in this same folder)
- It will play when she clicks "Yes"
- Background music continues playing along with fireworks

## Supported Formats
- MP3, MP4, M4A, OGG, WAV

## How to Add Files
1. Copy your audio files to: `valentine-gift/public/music/`
2. Rename background song to: `our-song.mp3`
3. Rename fireworks sound to: `fireworks.mp3`
